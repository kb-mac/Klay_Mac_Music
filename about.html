About Klay Mac

History

My name is Klayton MacDonnell and I love making and shaping music to share with everyone. I am a Canadian artist who likes to play energetic music that makes people move! Music that ignites an unstoppable force pulling you to the dance floor. I play a variety of Tech House, UKG/Breakbeats, Drum and Bass mixed with familiar vocals. Lots of energy, bass and dancing coming from behind the booth, let's have some fun!
